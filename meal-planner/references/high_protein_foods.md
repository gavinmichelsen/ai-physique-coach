# High-Protein Foods Reference

Use this list to help clients hit their protein targets.

## Lean Meats & Poultry
- Chicken Breast
- Turkey Breast
- Lean Ground Beef (93/7 or leaner)
- Pork Tenderloin
- Venison / Bison

## Seafood
- White Fish (Cod, Tilapia, Halibut)
- Fatty Fish (Salmon, Mackerel - higher fat)
- Shrimp / Prawns
- Tuna (Canned in water or fresh)

## Dairy & Eggs
- Egg Whites (Pure protein)
- Whole Eggs (Protein + Fat)
- Greek Yogurt (Non-fat or low-fat)
- Cottage Cheese (Low-fat)
- Whey / Casein Protein Powder

## Plant-Based
- Tofu / Tempeh
- Seitan
- Edamame
- Lentils / Beans (Significant carb content)
- Plant-based Protein Powders (Soy, Pea, Rice)

## Quick "Lazy" Protein
- Deli Turkey / Roast Beef
- Rotisserie Chicken (Skinless)
- Beef Jerky (Watch sugar/sodium)
- Pre-cooked Hard Boiled Eggs
