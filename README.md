## Overview

This repository contains a modular skills library designed to provide holistic physique coaching through AI. Each skill file represents a specific coaching capability that can be integrated into an AI coaching system.

## Skills Included

### Onboarding & Assessment
- **physique-coach-onboarding.skill** - Complete client intake and onboarding process
- **coaching-info.skill** - Overview of coaching functionality and skill coordination
- **goal-setting-milestones.skill** - Setting and tracking fitness goals

### Training Programs
- **resistance-training-program.skill** - Personalized strength training programs
- **cardio-programming.skill** - Cardiovascular exercise planning
- **exercise-demonstration.skill** - Exercise technique and form guidance

### Nutrition & Diet
- **diet-recommendation.skill** - Personalized nutrition guidance
- **meal-planner.skill** - Meal planning and preparation
- **meal-photo-estimator.skill** - Analyze food photos for calorie and macro estimation
- **tdee-calculation.skill** - Total Daily Energy Expenditure calculation
- **supplements.skill** - Supplement recommendations and guidance

### Progress Tracking
- **daily-check-in.skill** - Daily progress monitoring
- **weekly-check-in.skill** - Weekly progress reviews
- **progress-photos.skill** - Visual progress tracking
- **excel-tracker.skill** - Spreadsheet-based tracking tools

### Recovery & Adaptation
- **deload-fatigue-management.skill** - Managing training fatigue and recovery
- **sleep-stress-check-in.skill** - Sleep quality and stress monitoring
- **injury-pain-modification.skill** - Exercise modifications for injuries

### Lifestyle & Support
- **lifestyle-integration.skill** - Integrating fitness into daily life
- **travel-accommodations.skill** - Maintaining fitness while traveling
- **motivation-accountability.skill** - Motivation and accountability support

### Program Management
- **program-update.skill** - Updating and adjusting training programs
- **phase-transition.skill** - Transitioning between training phases

## Usage

Each `.skill` file contains structured instructions and capabilities that can be loaded into an AI coaching system to provide specialized guidance in that area.
